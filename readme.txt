== Description ==

Statly Analytics provides cutting edge analytics, sales funnel tracking and patent-pending, AI-based traffic mapping to help you generate more revenue and provide a better user experience for your visitors.  Aside from mapping your visitors journey to a purchase, you can do comprehensive sales funnel tracking to optimize every step of your sales process.  Statly also sends notifications when events (or pages) are performing better or worse than expected.

As an additional feature of the Statly Analytics plugin, you can add UTM variables to every link on your website automatically to track referring posts and pages; both in Statly and other analytics applications.

= Main Features: =
* Easy to setup and use. You don't need to be a programmer to use or understand Statly.
* No code to install. Once the plugin is set up, Statly code is added to your web pages and it's crunching your data.
* Advanced visitor tracking of all pages of your website, including each event in your sales funnel.
* The Statly Plugin automatically appends utm parameter to urls in wordpress posts
* Get a comprehensive look at your website traffic, customers and prospects.

= Why we need this plugin? =

* The Statly Plugin is built to help you optimize your sales process, from the inside out.
* Once the plugin is set up, it'll track where your visitors are coming from and where they're going...
* Making URLs trackable in Statly Analytics and other analytics platforms
* Monitoring sales and conversions of your coaching programs, services and products right out fo the box!
* Tracking visitors as they go through your sales funnel, step by step.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'Statly Analytics'
3. Click on 'Install Now'
4. Activate Statly Analytics from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download Statly Analytics.
2. Unzip plugin.
2. Upload the 'Statly Analytics' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate Statly Analytics from your Plugins page.


= Uninstalling: =

1. In the Admin Panel, go to "Plugins" and deactivate the plugin.
2. Delete the plugin from Plugins page.
